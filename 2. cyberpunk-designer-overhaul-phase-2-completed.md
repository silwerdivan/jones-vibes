# Phase 2: The Subscription Economy & Burnout Mechanics

## Overview
This phase introduces the core "Poverty Trap" dynamics described in the design documents. We will replace the generic "Rent" (currently represented by `DAILY_EXPENSE` and "Hab-Unit maintenance" strings) with an oppressive weekly "Burn Rate" (Rent + Oxygen Tax + Synth-Protein) and introduce a punishing "Forced Reboot" mechanic when Sanity (currently `happiness`) drops to 0.
