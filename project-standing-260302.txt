# Project Standing - March 2, 2026

## Overview
**Genre:** Cyberpunk Life Simulation / Strategy (1-2 Players, Hotseat or AI)
**Core Hook:** "The Math of Life" meets "Dystopian OS." A high-stakes balancing act of time, money, and biological needs, now fully rebranded as the **OmniLife OS** experience.

Items are no longer just flavor text; they are active mathematical modifiers.
*   **Omni-Chill (Fridge):** Reduces the base Bio-Deficit increase rate by 50% (+10 per cycle instead of +20).
*   **Hypno-Screen (Television):** Grants a +10% bonus to the new base "Cycle Rest" morale gain.
*   **Computer (Omni-Terminal):** Provides a +25% Study Speed bonus (10 credits per session instead of 8).

### üßò **Base Cycle Systems**
*   **Cycle Rest:** All players now receive a base +10 Morale gain at the end of each turn, representing mandatory downtime.
*   **Bio-Deficit Penalty:** Failing to maintain your biological needs now results in a "Bio-Deficit Penalty" to your Morale Quota.

### ‚è∞ **The Clock & Persistence**
*   **Time Management:** 24 hours per cycle. Strategic travel is key (Car cuts travel time by 50%).
*   **Seamless Persistence:** The game state (including AI "thinking" and open modals) is saved to SessionStorage.

## UI & UX Enhancements
*   **Clinical UI:** HUD and gauges have been updated with new labels and status chips (`Optimal`, `Deficit Warning`, `CRITICAL DEFICIT`).
*   **Auto-Arrival Dashboards:** Arriving at a location automatically triggers its interaction menu.
*   **Non-Blocking Notifications:** Dystopian log messages (e.g., "Hab-Pod maintenance deducted") appear in the top-mounted event strip.

---

## Current Limitations & "Gaps" (Feedback Received)
While the core lexicon is updated, several "immersion breakers" remain:
*   **Generic Jobs/Courses:** Job titles like "Dishwasher" and degrees like "Associate's" still need clinical rebranding (e.g., "Sanitation Technician 3rd Class").
*   **Remaining Items:** `Coffee`, `Movie Ticket`, and `New Clothes` are still generic and require functional buffs.
*   **Currency Symbol:** Still using `$`; needs transition to `OC` or a custom icon.
*   **Mascot (C.A.S.H.):** The mascot state machine logic (Happy/Angry/Greedy) is not yet implemented.
